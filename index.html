Simply awesome rally manager game

Dirt Rally is a persistent browser-based game (PBBG) for rally fans, where you get to control and influence a rally team. Starting out with a really basic rally car, drivers & co-drivers, crew and mechanics, you can try and win your way through to the top to become the best team and win the World Class group trophy.
